# **App Name**: RosaCash

## Core Features:

- Unified Transaction Entry: Record general income/expenses and credit card expenditures with details like date, category, subcategory, description, and value, seamlessly integrating credit card expenses into the overall financial picture.
- Dynamic Financial Summaries: Visualize summarized income, total expenses, and net profit for specific periods (daily, monthly, yearly) to monitor financial health.
- Category & Subcategory Management: Create, edit, and assign custom colors to spending categories and their respective subcategories for organized tracking and visual differentiation.
- Automated Recurring Expenses: Define and manage fixed, repetitive expenses (e.g., monthly bills) with automatic recurrence, streamlining future financial planning.
- Predictive Balance Viewer: View a real-time projection of your cash balance for any user-specified future date, dynamically accounting for recorded and recurring transactions.
- Interactive Spending Dashboard: An engaging visual dashboard presenting colorful charts and graphs to illustrate expense distribution by category, identify spending trends, and provide financial insights at a glance.
- Intelligent Transaction Categorization Tool: Utilize an AI-powered tool to suggest and automatically apply categories/subcategories to new transactions based on their descriptions and your historical categorization patterns, simplifying data entry.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary accent color: A warm and inviting pink (#E87DC0) to convey a feminine and modern feel, balancing boldness with elegance.
- Background color: A very light, subtle pink-tinged white (#F5F0F2) to create a clean, airy, and gentle backdrop that enhances readability and keeps the interface feeling fresh.
- Accent color: A rich and vibrant magenta-purple (#E052E0) chosen as an analogous complement to the primary, providing strong contrast for calls-to-action and important highlights.
- Headlines and body text font: 'Inter', a clean and versatile grotesque-style sans-serif, chosen for its excellent readability and modern, objective aesthetic suitable for financial data.
- Use clear, simple, and color-coded icons to represent categories and transaction types, enhancing visual organization and quick identification across the dashboard and transaction lists.
- A clean, card-based layout with clear hierarchy to present financial data. Dashboards will emphasize easy-to-read charts and numerical summaries, making information accessible at a glance.
- Subtle, smooth transitions for navigations and data updates, such as filtering periods or adding new transactions, to provide a responsive and pleasant user experience without distraction.
